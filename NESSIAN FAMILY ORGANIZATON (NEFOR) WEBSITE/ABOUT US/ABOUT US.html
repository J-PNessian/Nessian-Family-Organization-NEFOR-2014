HISTORY OF THE FAMILY

Generally, the African history was not written so many years ago, but it was based on oral history, such as Battle songs, Tile , story told by grandparents to their children.
Because there was not a written history, most of the family history lost when the elder with in the family dies at their early age. Therefore, this Nessian family history was just twice not long ago. Meanwhile, the great grandparents that this history beginning from was called Gbanquoi Bolomen.


The great grandparents of the Nessian family decenden from the Town called Zuapea which is present day part of Guinea. At that time that those parents were in Zuapea, this town was part of Liberia. The Nessian family believed to be a Zuo for the Polo society. This society is a male society that ----in Liberia for man education. During the time of the inesuciation, in the town called Gipo Township, the Nessian family which believed to be the Zuo family, trained along this Polo society never preson and settle in Gipo, Township Ghannah Clan Nimba County District #8.
